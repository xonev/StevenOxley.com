As I mentioned in [Are Migrations Good for Your Career?](https://www.stevenoxley.com/blog/2025/10/29/are-migrations-good-for-your-career/), "naming things" is notoriously one of the hard problems of Computer Science. But naming things can also be fun! What is the best name that you've come up with at work? The name I'm proudest of so far would have to be "Sk8s" (Seeq on Kubernetes, pronounced "Skates"). People have really run with it, too, with follow-on names like "Sk8park" and "Sk8board."
