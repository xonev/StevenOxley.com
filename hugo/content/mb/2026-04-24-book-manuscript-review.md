If you like learning via books like me, you might be interested in reviewing manuscripts prior to publishing. It can be fun because you can help shape the book and also learn about the topic.

Here are some of the books I've had the pleasure of reviewing:

- [Infrastructure as Code, Patterns and Practices](https://www.manning.com/books/infrastructure-as-code-patterns-and-practices)
- [Core Data in Swift](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/core-data-in/9781680502046/f_0004.xhtml)
- [Grokking Streaming Systems](https://www.manning.com/books/grokking-streaming-systems)

I was even quoted inside the cover of **Core Data in Swift** 🙀
